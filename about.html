About Pt.JSRDIC

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Pt.JSRDIC has been at the forefront of technology education, empowering thousands of students to launch successful careers in web development and design. Our institute was born from a passion for technology and a commitment to making quality education accessible to everyone.

We believe that practical, hands-on learning is the most effective way to master web technologies. That's why our curriculum is designed around real-world projects and industry best practices.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical tech skills that transform careers and lives through innovative education.

Our Vision

To become the leading institute for web technology education, recognized for excellence and innovation.

Our Impact

Over 10,000 students trained with 95% placement rate in top tech companies worldwide.
